Welcome to Weighryte Store

We partner with manufacturers of electronic truck weigh bridges and weighing systems who have pioneered the design, development and manufacturing of weigh bridges in conformity with the Indian legal metrology regulations.

The manufacturers have their own factory setup with all amenities required to manufacture electronic truck weigh bridges at industrial development areas.

Our sales and service providers have skilled professionals with multiple years of proven in designing systems of software as per customer requirements. There are exclusive departments to give prompt services to the esteemed customers. Our partners constantly deal with the demand and supply of weighing systems along with exporting an array of weigh bridges.

The product is manufactured from the scratch which starts with initializing the best raw material. A lot of time and effort has been invested to find trust worthy vender base which is helping to produce only factory tested raw material.

Also we and our partners have constant interactions with customers to know their requirements and the accurate specification of the products and services the customer needs. Because of this we and our partners have a very solid reputation in the industry along with having numerous customers across the country. For further details please contact us.
